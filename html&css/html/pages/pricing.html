Addiction: Types, Causes, and Solutions (For Teens)

The reality of addiction is no joke! An addiction can be to a substance or a behavior. The difference between really liking something and being addicted are obsessive thoughts, feeling like you can't stop, and continuing to use or do that substance or behavior despite having negative consequences.
